The Best Movies According To Evan

My top 5 movies of all-time

Interstellar

The idea behind this movie is really good. I enjoy the plot and the original soundtrack

Tresure Planet

This was my childhood favorite movie because I enjoyed the imagination behind it. The characters are well written for a Disney movie!

Tron Legacy

I know I probably should enjoy the original more than the sequal but I enjoy the more modern films.

Spider-Man Into The Spider-Verse

Great movie overall, its nice to have a different Spider-Man other than Peter Parker. The story was fantastic and the soundtrack was also good.

Ready Player One

Just like gamers had their favorite movies from back in the day, I believe this will be the one for modern times.
